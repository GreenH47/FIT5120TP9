Turn a plastic bottle into a cute little cow coin storage!

Materials:

Plastic bottle
Recycled plastic
Foam sheet (white, pink, black)
Yarn (40cm) x3
Black flannel
White paper
Plastic eyes

Tools:

Cutter
Glue gun
Scissor
Curve shape scissor
Marker

Steps:

1. Prepare a plastic bottle and remove the sticker.

2. If the bottle is too long, use scissors to cut the bottom to cut the middle of the bottle out.

3. Cut the bottle cap part using cutter.

4. Combine the top and bottom parts of the bottle.

5. Use a marker to mark the coin-shaped rectangle in the centre of the bottle and cut it out.

6. Use types to connect the top and bottom parts of the bottle.

7. Cut a proper size of pink and white form sheet and use a glue gun to attach it to the bottle cap. Make it the nose of the cow.

8. Cut a coin-shaped hole on a white form sheet and use glue to attach it to the bottle as a cow's body.

9. Use a pink and white form sheet to make a pair of ears, and use a black form sheet to make a pair of horns and attach it to the bottle.

10. Round the yarn with two fingers 10 times and tie with short yarn, make 3 of it and attach to the bottle as cow hair.

11. Cutting the black form sheet into random shapes and stick it to the cow's body as patterns.

12. Rowling 4 white form sheets as legs and use curve shape scissors to cut the black form sheet into patterns of legs, and attach the legs to the body.

13. Cut the paper into 2 round shapes and stick the plastic eyes on it to make it a cow's eyes and finally stick the eyes to the bottle. Finish!!
