Landfill bin is used to collect and dispose of waste that is not recyclable or compostable. Once full, the contents of the bin are taken to a landfill site, where they are buried and left to decompose over time. Using landfill bins helps in segregating waste, ensuring that only non-recyclable materials end up in landfills, while recyclable and organic materials can be processed separately.
Recycle bin is designed to collect materials that can be processed and reused, reducing the need for raw materials and conserving resources.
Green bin is used to collect organic waste, such as food scraps and yard trimmings. These materials are then composted, turning them into nutrient-rich soil additives.
