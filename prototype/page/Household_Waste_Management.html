Reducing Plastic Waste

Plastic waste has become a major environmental concern. By reducing our usage of single-use plastics and opting for reusable alternatives, we can significantly reduce plastic waste going into landfills and ending up in our oceans.

Composting Food Waste

Composting food waste is an eco-friendly way to reduce landfill waste and create nutrient-rich soil. Simply collect your food scraps and garden waste in a compost bin, and let nature do the work to create compost that can be used in your own garden.

Recycling Paper & Cardboard

Recycling paper and cardboard is an effective way to minimize deforestation and reduce energy consumption. Make sure to separate paper and cardboard from other wastes and place them in designated recycling bins.
